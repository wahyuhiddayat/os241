## WEEK 02

* [Learn Regular Expressions (Regex) - Crash Course for Beginners](https://youtu.be/ZfQFUJhPqMM?si=Y5ceOLe-EO0y4ddq)
A comprehensive guide to understanding and using Regular Expressions (Regex).
